# FANUC India Demo Script

## Opening line

“This is not a dashboard. This is an AI operating layer across vision, robot telemetry, maintenance, knowledge and work execution.”

## 7-minute flow

### 1. Command Center
Show OEE, active robots, downtime risk, quality yield and open AI alerts.

### 2. Start Live Scenario
Click **Start live scenario**.

### 3. Vision AI
The inspection video runs. The system highlights a surface anomaly with a bounding box, confidence score and recommended action.

### 4. Robot Telemetry
Move to Robot Telemetry. Show torque, vibration and temperature drifting on Robot R-07.

### 5. Predictive Maintenance
Show high risk and remaining useful life. Explain that AIonOS is connecting quality events with asset health.

### 6. SOP Copilot
Ask: “Show SOP for gripper calibration.” The copilot returns steps from the synthetic SOP library.

### 7. Work Orders
Create the AI work order. Show priority, owner, SOP link, business impact and connected defect events.

## Closing line

“Automation tells the robot what to do. AIonOS tells the enterprise what is happening, why it is happening, what will happen next, and what to do about it.”
